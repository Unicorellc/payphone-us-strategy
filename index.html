Risk Analysis

Key Risks & Mitigations

Proactive identification and management of market entry risks.

Regulatory Complexity

MTL licensing in 50 states is expensive and time-consuming. Mitigation: Partner with licensed sponsor bank; prioritize high-volume states first.

Competitive Response

Incumbents may match pricing or acquire competitors. Mitigation: Build ecosystem moat with super-app strategy; focus on community loyalty.

Compliance & AML

U.S. AML/KYC requirements are stringent. Mitigation: Invest in robust compliance tech; hire experienced compliance team.

Customer Acquisition Cost

CAC in competitive market may exceed projections. Mitigation: Community-led growth; referral programs; leverage Ecuador brand.

FX Rate Volatility

Currency fluctuations impact margins. Mitigation: Hedging strategies; real-time pricing; Ecuador uses USD (no FX risk).

Technology Scalability

Platform must handle U.S. volume. Mitigation: Cloud infrastructure; proven tech stack from Ecuador operations.
